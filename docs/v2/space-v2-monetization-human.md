# Space v2.0 Monetization and Reputation Concept

## Overview
Version 2.0 introduces a layered system that combines user reputation, premium subscriptions, and curated discovery rewards. The goal is to encourage high-quality user contributions while creating a sustainable monetization model that still feels useful and fair.

## Product Goals
- Reward users for contributing valuable places to the map.
- Encourage repeat engagement through points, streaks, and unlocks.
- Offer premium benefits without making the product feel paywalled.
- Improve content quality by rewarding useful contributions instead of raw volume.

## Core Idea
Users earn points for meaningful actions, especially adding new places that pass moderation or receive community validation. Those points can be used to unlock curated "hidden gems" selected by admins. Premium subscribers receive additional daily or weekly unlocks, broader access to hidden gems, and faster progression.

## User Reputation Model
A single score is not enough, so the system should track several dimensions:
- Contribution score: number of approved additions and edits.
- Trust score: quality and reliability of contributions.
- Discovery score: useful places discovered or unlocked.
- Community score: likes, saves, confirmations, and helpful activity.

These scores can power visible badges, profile levels, and ranking lists.

## Points and Rewards
Points should be granted for quality, not just quantity. Examples:
- Adding a new place that is approved.
- Completing a full place profile with description, category, and location.
- Uploading useful photos or details.
- Confirming a duplicate or invalid place.
- Receiving positive feedback from other users.

To prevent abuse, points should be limited or reduced for repetitive actions and low-value contributions.

## Hidden Gems System
Hidden gems are curated places added by admins and shown as special content. Users can unlock them in different ways:
- By spending points.
- By completing daily tasks.
- Through premium subscription access.
- As part of weekly or seasonal rewards.

Hidden gems can be organized by category, location, theme, or randomly across all categories.

## Subscription Model
Premium subscription should enhance discovery, not block the core product. Suggested benefits:
- Daily hidden gem unlocks.
- Larger monthly or weekly unlock quota.
- Access to more categories or random all-category drops.
- Priority access to newly curated hidden gems.
- Extra reputation boosts or bonus points for verified activity.

A free tier should still remain useful, with limited but meaningful access.

## Gamification Features
Additional systems that fit well with this model:
- Daily streaks for active users.
- Weekly missions such as adding places or completing profiles.
- Category mastery progress.
- Seasonal leaderboards.
- Badges like Explorer, Trusted Contributor, or Hidden Gem Hunter.
- Referral rewards for bringing in new users.

## Anti-Abuse Rules
The system needs clear protection against spam and gaming:
- Only award points after moderation or validation.
- Reduce rewards for duplicate, low-quality, or repeated submissions.
- Add daily limits for point farming.
- Track suspicious activity and temporarily suspend rewards when needed.
- Make premium benefits about convenience and discovery, not about taking over the product.

## Risks
The biggest risks are spam, content dilution, and making the product feel too paywalled. To avoid this, the platform should keep some hidden gems free, reward quality over volume, and use subscriptions mainly to improve speed, depth, and convenience.

## Recommended v2.0 Direction
The best order of implementation is:
1. Reputation and points system.
2. Hidden gems unlock flow.
3. Premium subscription benefits.
4. Badges, leaderboards, and streaks.
5. Seasonal campaigns and special collections.

## Decision Summary
This idea is strong if the product treats points as a reputation system and subscriptions as discovery enhancements. In that form, v2.0 can improve user retention, content quality, and monetization at the same time.
